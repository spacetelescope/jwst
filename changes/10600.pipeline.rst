Streamlined stage3 spectral processing inputs for WFSS mode
by enabling the Spec3Pipeline to take 1-D extracted spectra (x1d)
instead of cal files, bypassing identical calls to pixel_replace
and extract_1d steps between the spec2 and spec3 pipelines for
those data. As a result, the runtime could be improved by
at least 2x (just a rough estimate as runtime is very machine-dependent).
